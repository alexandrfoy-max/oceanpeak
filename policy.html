Booking & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 2026

🏨 Private Trips

Advance payment:

  • Sailing yacht (Lady Sunshine): €200 deposit
  • Motorboats (Champagne / Armani): €150 deposit
  • Or 100% payment at the time of booking

Balance payment: On board – cash or card (7% surcharge for card payments).

👥 Shared Trips

100% payment required at the time of booking.

⛵ Inter-Island Charters

  • 30% at the time of booking
  • 40% one month before departure (non-refundable deposit)
  • Remaining 30% + APA paid 48 hours before departure

❌ Cancellation Policy

Weather / Technical Issues

If the yacht is technically out of service on the day of the trip or weather conditions do not allow departure:

  • Trip rescheduled (subject to confirmation) OR
  • 100% refund of the deposit

Client Cancellation – Private Trips

If cancelled less than 72 hours before departure: deposit is non-refundable.

Client Cancellation – Shared Trips

If cancelled less than 48 hours before departure: deposit is non-refundable.

Inter-Island Charter Cancellation

If cancelled less than 14 days before departure: amount paid is non-refundable.

Refund processing time: 10 business days.

💳 Card Payment Surcharge

For all on-board card payments, an additional 7% surcharge will be applied to the outstanding balance.
