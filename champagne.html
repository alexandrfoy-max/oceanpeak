The Experience

Champagne is an elegant Sealine SC29 motor boat designed for stylish cruising along Tenerife’s southern coastline. Departing from Puerto Colón, guests can discover volcanic cliffs, sea caves and natural swimming spots while enjoying views of Mount Teide from the sea.
